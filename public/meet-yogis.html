For the sake of happiness and peace the Metta Yogis have joined the blockchain.

What is a Metta Yogi?

A Yogi is a person who strives to discover his innate qualities. Every Metta Yogi is motivated by Metta (word in pali for loving-kindness), the sincere wish that all beings have happiness and peace.

The Art

The art is inspired by the ancient himalayan tradition of Thangka painting, in particular the karma sgar-ris or the Karmapa’s tent encampment style that was initiated by the great artist Namkha Tashi (c. 16th century) inspired by stylistic elements from India, China, Persia and Tibet.

Lay Yogis

Familiar with worldly affairs, the Lay Yogi lives in the city, the countryside, in urban dwellings and other places where the modern complexities of life exist. Lay Yogis are professionals, creatives, come from all classes of society and are connected with people throughout the world.

Ascetic Yogis

With their years of experience in the forests and caves, their abilities are heightened and precise. They have clear vision and mental stability. They descend down into the city and offer mantras and prayers to help improve conditions for those in the world.

Renunciant Yogis

A religious way of life in which the monk renounces worldly pursuits to devote oneself fully to spiritual work.

Accomplished Yogis

A yogi who, through the practice of sādhanā, attains the realization of siddhis, psychic and spiritual abilities and powers.

Contemporary features and attributes have been added to the Metta Yogis to create unique pieces bridging cultures and times. Originally Thangka paintings were reserved to illustrate the sacred Buddhist figures alone and therefore never destined to be sold.

A Metta Yogi, however, stands for his owner. Every significant trait symbolizes a potential or a quality that can be developed.

Every one of the traits were meticulously hand drawn. All possible combinations of the traits could produce several million unique NFTs, however only 888 will be minted from traits combined by an algorithm.

Benefits of having a Metta Yogi

With their limitless loving kindness and compassion, the Metta Yogis appear from space to help free them from suffering.

Earn merit tokens to build and develop your qualities, benefit the world and play the game of rebirth, karma and the stages of the path to Enlightenment.

Playing the game helps people learn about the Buddhist path to eliminate suffering. Each step in the game is one step closer to winning the game, or enlightenment. Participate in concrete charitable actions and help build a supportive community.

How to play

To play the game you will first need to get an NFT Metta Yogi. On launch 888 yogis will be minted giving players a chance to get started from the early.

The cost of a Metta Yogi NFT will be kept at a low price making it easy and inexpensive to play the game, normally within the reasonable range of 20-80 USD.

If one misses out on the mint, new and more special characters will be auctioned off at various times, post mint.

The MettaYogi NFT will have a random set of attributes and characteristics that will factor into game play later.
